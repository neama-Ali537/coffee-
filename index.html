Specialty Coffee

Specialty coffee is fresh coffee that is carefully processed and shipped, of known origin, the height at which it was grown, as well as the date of roasting, also respecting the correct standards and methods of preparation. All this contributes to the creation of a cup, rich in distinctive aromas and flavors, and this distinguishes it from other types of commercial coffee.

Proud of Quality

We improve the quality of specialty coffee being offered in the UAE market. It is the highest grade of coffee available, typically relating to the entire supply chain, using single origin or single estate coffee beans. Usually growing above 1000 m from the sea, always freshly medium roasted from the best world’s plantation.
